Why Sakoflex?

KYC Compliance

Compliance with the sector and/or Central Bank rules, policies & laws on KYC.




Risk Mitigation

Multifactor authentication for the system, this helps eliminate risks of hacking, illegal access to the system, and/or unauthorized access to data in the system.


workflow

Maker-Checker workflow support.Inbuilt continuous security monitoring and improvements.Sequential & rule-based workflow for routing & escalation of loan applications. Bi-directional flow is between authorized system users.

E-forms

Document management feature which enables customer to upload document into the system such as Microsoft word, PDF, JPEG or PNG. Loan application form with credit score engine for appraisal. Instant customer feedback on qualification reduce on paperwork.


Mobility

“B2C”model; business goes to customer. Offline Mode: Enables agents to “hunt” for customers anywhere and sync their transactions with the central server later. “Anytime-Anywhere” philosophy; boosts numbers, values & customer satisfaction index.


Notifications

SACCO Benki has an integrated bulk SMS module. This combined with email notifications feature helps SACCO deliver real time notifications to members. Email notifications are sent to the transacting users at set stages of the process workflow. SMS notifications are sent to the customer, to all networks and across borders.
